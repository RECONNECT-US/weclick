Support Guide

  1. Help the learner open their internet browser and search for Citizens Advice.
  2. Show them how to choose the correct section for their issue, such as money, housing, benefits, work, family or law.
  3. Encourage them to read headings carefully and scroll slowly so they can find the right advice page.
  4. Show them how to go back, open useful pages, and save or write down important information.
  5. If needed, help them find local contact details or support routes from the website.
  • Can the learner open a browser?
  • Can the learner search for Citizens Advice?
  • Can the learner find the correct topic page?
  • Can the learner read and understand the information?
  • Can the learner save, note or return to the page later?
Helpful reminder: keep the session practical and calm. The aim is to build confidence, not rush. Let the learner try each step where possible.
