Learning Points

Tip 1: show the learner how to search carefully and recognise the official GOV.UK website.
Tip 2: encourage them to read page titles and check they are on the right service before entering information.
Tip 3: let them practise filling boxes, choosing options and moving between pages themselves where possible.

  • Can the learner find the correct service?
  • Can the learner understand the questions being asked?
  • Can the learner enter information safely and accurately?
  • Can the learner save or make a note of what they have done?
