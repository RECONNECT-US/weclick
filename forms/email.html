Email Help Support

Use this worksheet to support learners with setting up, accessing and using email. This can include creating an account, signing in, sending messages, replying, opening attachments and understanding safe email use.

Sample worksheet only: this page is for guidance and to show how the form is laid out. It does not collect, store or submit any personal information, and it cannot be filled in online.

Learning Guide

  1. Explain what an email address is and how it is used for contact, accounts, and online services.
  2. Show the learner how to sign in safely and keep their password private.
  3. Practise opening the inbox, reading a message, replying, and sending a new email.
  4. Go through useful features such as search, attachments, deleted items, and spam or junk folders.
  5. Encourage the learner to send simple practice emails so they get used to the process.
  • Can the learner sign in safely?
  • Can the learner read and reply to an email?
  • Can the learner send a new message?
  • Can the learner recognise suspicious or unexpected emails?
  • Can the learner open or download safe attachments?
Good practice: email support should build confidence as well as safety awareness. Learners should know how to use email in everyday life without feeling rushed or overwhelmed.
