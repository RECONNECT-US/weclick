How to use this hub

Simple steps for learners, staff, or volunteers supporting job searches.

1. Add learner details.
Put the learner name in at the top so the hub feels personal and easier to manage.
2. Fill in preferences.
Add the job title, area, distance, salary, and whether the person wants full-time, part-time, permanent, or remote work.
3. Save a profile.
Use a profile name such as “Admin jobs Kent” or “Part-time care roles” so it can be loaded again later.
4. Build and open results.
The hub creates search links for five UK job sites and opens them in separate tabs.
CV and interview help:
On the right-hand side you will see quick support prompts to help the learner prepare before applying.

Support reminders

Good match check: make sure the job title, travel distance, and salary are realistic for the learner.
Before applying: check spelling, contact details, work history, and whether the learner needs help with online forms.
After applying: note where the application was sent and whether follow-up support is needed.
Important: each website has its own filters and may not honour every option exactly the same way. This hub aims to send people to the nearest matching search page quickly and simply.
