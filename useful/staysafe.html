🛡️ Stay Safe Online

Check URLs, HTTPS & Pop-up Warnings

🔍 Verifying URLs

  • Always check the full web address before clicking.
  • Look for spelling mistakes (e.g. faceboook.com).
  • Watch out for extra words or numbers (e.g. paypal-login123.com).
  • Use Google or Bing instead of clicking links in emails or messages.

🔒 HTTPS & Secure Websites

  • Look for the padlock symbol 🔒.
  • Secure websites begin with https://
  • The padlock does NOT guarantee safety.
  • Avoid entering details on sites without HTTPS.

👀 Real vs Fake Website Example

✅ Real Website

https://www.paypal.com

  • Correct spelling
  • Uses HTTPS
  • No strange extras

⚠️ Fake Website

http://paypal-login123.net

  • Extra words
  • Strange domain
  • No HTTPS

🚫 Pop-up Warning Signs

  • “Your computer is infected!”
  • Requests for payment or prizes
  • Pop-ups asking for login details
  • Can’t close normally
  • Bad spelling or grammar

✅ Quick Safety Tips

  • Hover over links before clicking
  • If unsure, search manually
  • Never download from pop-ups
  • Ask for help if unsure
