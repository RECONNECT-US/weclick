🏦 Guide to Accessing Banking Apps

Banking apps can help you check your balance, manage your money and make payments safely. This guide explains what you need, how to set one up, and how to use it with confidence.

Purpose of this guide:

To help WECLICK clients confidently access and use banking apps on a smartphone or tablet to check balances, manage money and make payments safely.

✅ What You Will Need

  • A smartphone or tablet (Apple or Android)
  • An active bank account with online banking enabled
  • An internet connection (Wi-Fi or mobile data)
  • Your bank card or account details
  • Photo ID if your bank asks for identity checks
  • An email address and/or mobile number registered with your bank

⬇️ Downloading Your Bank’s App

  1. Open the App Store on iPhone or Google Play Store on Android
  2. Search for your bank’s official app, such as Barclays, NatWest or Lloyds Bank
  3. Check the publisher is the actual bank
  4. Look at reviews and download numbers
  5. Tap Download or Install
  6. Open the app when it finishes installing

🔍 How to Check It Is the Official App

🏷️
Check the Name Make sure the app name matches your bank
✅
Check the Publisher The publisher should be your bank, not a random company
⭐
Check Reviews Read reviews and confirm it looks genuine

📝 Setting Up the App

  1. Open the banking app
  2. Select Register or Set up online banking
  3. Enter your bank details, such as sort code, account number or card number
  4. Verify your identity using a text code, a phone call or photo ID if requested
  5. Create your login details
  6. Set up extra security such as a PIN, fingerprint or Face ID if offered

📱 What You Can Do in the App

  • Check your balance and transactions
  • Transfer money between your own accounts
  • Send money to other people
  • Pay bills
  • Set up standing orders or direct debits
  • View or download statements

🛡️ Security Tips

  • Never share your PIN, password or one-time codes
  • Only download banking apps from official app stores
  • Update the app regularly
  • Log out after use if the app does not do this automatically
  • If you lose your phone, contact your bank straight away

🔧 Common Problems & Solutions

Problem Possible Fix
Can’t log in Check your username and password, then reset them if needed
App won’t open Update the app or restart your device
Not receiving text codes Check your phone signal and confirm your number is registered with the bank
Forgot password Use the Reset password option in the app

⚠️ Important Reminder

A real bank will never ask you to share your full password, PIN or one-time passcode with someone else. If a message or call asks for this, stop and contact your bank directly.

🤝 Getting More Help

  • Visit your local bank branch for in-person support
  • Call your bank’s customer service number on the back of your bank card
  • Ask your WECLICK support worker to guide you through the steps
