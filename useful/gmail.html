📧 Guide to Accessing a Gmail Account

Gmail is Google’s email service. It lets you send and receive emails, store files, and access tools like Google Drive, Docs and Calendar.

What Gmail can be used for:

Sending emails, storing files in Google Drive, accessing documents, and managing your calendar and contacts.

📝 Step 1: Creating a Gmail Account

  1. Go to www.gmail.com
  2. Click Create account
  3. Choose For myself
  4. Enter your name and choose a Gmail address
  5. Create a strong password
  6. Add your phone number (recommended for security)
  7. Enter your date of birth and other details
  8. Follow the steps to finish creating your account

🔐 Step 2: Signing In

  1. Go to www.gmail.com
  2. Enter your email address and password
  3. Use 2-step verification if set up
  4. Only stay signed in on your own device

🛡️ Security Tips

  • Never share your password
  • Turn on 2-step verification
  • Check email addresses before clicking links
  • Sign out on shared devices

📥 Step 4: Checking Your Inbox

  • Click an email to read it

🗂️ Step 5: Staying Organised

  • Use labels to organise emails
  • Use the search bar to find emails quickly
  • Archive or delete emails you don’t need

💡 Helpful Reminder

Gmail also gives you access to:

  • Google Drive for file storage
  • Google Docs for documents
  • Google Calendar for appointments
