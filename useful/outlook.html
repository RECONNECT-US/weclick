📧 Guide to Accessing an Outlook Account

Outlook lets you send and receive emails, stay organised, and access Microsoft services like OneDrive, Calendar and Office online apps.

What Outlook can be used for:

Sending emails, checking your inbox, storing files in OneDrive, using your calendar, and accessing Microsoft tools online.

📝 Step 1: Creating an Outlook Account

  1. Go to www.outlook.com
  2. Click Create free account
  3. Choose your email address name
  4. Select @outlook.com or @hotmail.com
  5. Create a strong password using letters, numbers and symbols
  6. Enter your first name, surname and date of birth
  7. Complete the security check to prove you are not a robot

🔐 Step 2: Signing In

  1. Visit www.outlook.com
  2. Enter your email address and password
  3. Tick Keep me signed in only on your own device
  4. Always sign out on shared or public computers

🛡️ Security Tips

  • Never share your password
  • Enable 2-step verification for extra security
  • Check sender addresses before opening links
  • Always sign out on shared devices

📥 Step 4: Checking Your Inbox

  • New emails appear in your Inbox
  • Click a message to open it
  • Use Reply, Reply All, or Forward when needed

🗂️ Step 5: Staying Organised

  • Create folders for important emails
  • Use the search bar to find messages quickly
  • Delete or archive emails you no longer need

💡 Helpful Reminder

Outlook is not just for email. It also gives you access to:

  • OneDrive for file storage
  • Calendar for appointments and reminders
  • Microsoft Office online apps
