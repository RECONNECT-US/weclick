💼 Guide to Using Indeed

Indeed is one of the UK’s largest job search websites. This guide shows you how to create an account, upload your CV, search for jobs, and apply with confidence.

What you can do on Indeed:

Search for jobs, upload your CV, apply online, and receive alerts for new job opportunities.

🌐 Step 1: Go to Indeed

  1. Open your internet browser
  2. Type www.indeed.co.uk
  3. Click Sign In if you already have an account
  4. Or click Create an account to get started

📝 Step 2: Create an Account

  • Use an email address you check regularly
  • Create a strong password
  • Confirm your email before continuing

📄 Step 3: Upload or Create Your CV

  • Upload your CV (Word or PDF)
  • Or build one directly on Indeed
  • Include work history, skills, and contact details
  • Keep it clear and up to date

🔎 Step 4: Search for Jobs

💬
Job Title Type a role like “Cleaner” or “Admin”
📍
Location Enter your town or postcode
🔍
Search Click “Find Jobs”

📤 Step 5: Apply for Jobs

  1. Click on a job title
  2. Read the full job description
  3. Click Apply Now
  4. Follow the steps to submit your application
  5. Some jobs may take you to the company’s own website

🔔 Step 6: Job Alerts

  • Save your searches
  • Turn on job alerts
  • Receive daily or weekly emails with new jobs

🛡️ Staying Safe

  • Be careful of scam job adverts
  • Never pay money to apply for a job
  • Do not share bank details unless you are sure the job is genuine
  • Research the company if unsure

💡 Top Tips

  • Apply for jobs daily
  • Keep your CV simple and professional
  • Use keywords from job descriptions
  • Check your email regularly
  • Respond quickly to employers
