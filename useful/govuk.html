🌐 Guide to Accessing GOV.UK

GOV.UK is the official UK Government website. This guide shows you how to access it safely, search for services, and find the information or forms you need.

What GOV.UK is used for:

GOV.UK gives access to official government information and services including benefits, tax, passports, driving, housing, legal matters, education and much more.

✅ What You Need

  • A smartphone, tablet, or computer
  • An internet connection
  • A web browser such as Chrome, Edge, Firefox, or Safari
  • Any personal documents needed for the service you want to use

🌍 What You Can Use GOV.UK For

  • Tax and benefits
  • Driving and transport
  • Passports, visas and immigration
  • Housing and local services
  • Births, deaths, marriages and care
  • Business and self-employment
  • Justice and the law
  • Education and learning

🧭 Step-by-Step: How to Access GOV.UK

  1. Open your web browser on your phone, tablet, or computer
  2. Click or tap the address bar at the top of the screen
  3. Type gov.uk
  4. Press Enter or tap Go
  5. Check the address says www.gov.uk
  6. Use the search box on the homepage to find the service or information you need

📄 Choosing the Right Page

  • Look for the official black and white GOV.UK heading
  • Use search results from inside GOV.UK where possible
  • Avoid ads or pop-ups from other websites
  • Read the page carefully before entering details

🛡️ Staying Safe on GOV.UK

  • Always check the website ends in .gov.uk
  • Never enter personal details unless you are sure it is the official website
  • Avoid public Wi-Fi when using sensitive services
  • Log out of any account when you finish

💡 Helpful Tips

  • GOV.UK is free to use
  • The website works on phones, tablets, and computers
  • Bookmark www.gov.uk so you can find it quickly next time
  • Take your time and follow the on-screen instructions carefully
