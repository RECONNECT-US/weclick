Example money-saving ideas

Helpful prompts for people facing financial pressure, especially where heating and essentials are a concern.

  • Check whether there are unused subscriptions or direct debits that can be paused or cancelled.
  • Set a weekly food budget and build meals around lower-cost basics already at home.
  • Review heating usage room by room and focus warmth where it is needed most.
  • Look at travel habits and compare walking, lift-sharing, bus passes, or off-peak options where realistic.
  • Pay priority bills first and be honest early if a payment plan or support conversation is needed.
  • Even a very small emergency saving amount can help stop future borrowing.
