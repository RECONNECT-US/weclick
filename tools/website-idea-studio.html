What makes a strong website subject?

A strong subject is clear, useful, and aimed at a specific audience. It should solve a problem, support a need, or make something easier to understand or access.
Good website ideas often include support services, learning tools, information hubs, campaigns, local groups, step-by-step guidance, or professional services.
Before building anything, try to answer three things: who is this for, what does it help with, and what should visitors do next?
